DEFINITIONS:

The Territorial Scope refers to the extent of the geographical jurisdiction within which DTI grants the business to only trade, buy, store, handle, manufacture, or process goods, or other business transactions.

The Disclaimer means the right to limit the use of certain words or enumerations of letters as numerals attached to your Business Name.

Example:
Business Name: Sari-Sari Store Juan
Description: Retail Store
Sector: Retail Juan Sari-Sari Store

Reminders:

  • Fields to fill up must have at least two or more words.
  • Not a term or word that is unlawful, immoral, or scandalous.
  • Not a registered trade name, trademark, or business name.
  • Not composed of purely generic words (e.g., Health Care Clinic).
  • Not misleading or deceptive.
  • Not the same as a government agency or international organization.
