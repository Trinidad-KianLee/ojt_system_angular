Application for the Philippine Standard License for Vaporized Nicotine and Non-Nicotine Products

The Philippine Standard License (PSL) is an official certification issued by the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI). It serves as a mark of compliance for manufacturers, importers, and distributors of vaporized nicotine and non-nicotine products, including e-cigarettes, heated tobacco products (HTPs), and other similar devices.

This license confirms that products meet the Philippine National Standards (PNS) or acceptable international standards for safety, quality, labeling, and performance. Obtaining a PSL is mandatory for any business intending to legally manufacture, import, distribute, or sell these products in the Philippines.

Whether you're a large manufacturer, a local distributor, or a small online seller, securing a PSL is a legal obligation under the Republic Act No. 11900 (Vape Law) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR). Failure to comply can result in penalties, product seizure, or business suspension.
