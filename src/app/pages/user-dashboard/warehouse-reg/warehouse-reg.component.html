Application for Certificate of Warehouse Registration

Comply with local regulations and ensure the safe storage of vaporized nicotine and non-nicotine products.

The application for the Certificate of Warehouse Registration is a mandatory requirement for any entity storing vaporized nicotine and non-nicotine products intended for distribution or sale.

This certification ensures that all storage facilities meet the required standards for safety, security, and regulatory compliance. By registering warehouses, the Office maintains oversight over the supply chain, helping to prevent the unauthorized distribution of products and ensuring that all stored items adhere to approved product specifications and legal guidelines.

In addition to fulfilling legal obligations, this process promotes accountability and transparency within the industry. Proper warehouse registration supports responsible product handling and strengthens efforts to protect public health, ensuring that only compliant and traceable products reach the market.
