Application for Registration of Age-Gating Mechanism

Ensuring responsible access and legal compliance for vaporized nicotine and non-nicotine products.

The application for the registration of the Age-Gating Mechanism is essential to ensure legal compliance with age restrictions for access to vaporized nicotine and non-nicotine products.

This mechanism is designed to verify the age of individuals accessing content or making purchases on our website, ensuring that only those who meet the legal age requirements are permitted to engage with restricted materials. By implementing this system, we uphold regulatory standards and protect public health by preventing underage exposure to such products.

In addition to fulfilling legal obligations, the system helps raise awareness about the importance of responsible consumption and the risks associated with these products. By implementing this mechanism, we not only protect minors but also promote a safer and more informed environment for adult consumers.
