Application for Certificate of Registration (COR)

for Wholesaler / Trader / Distributor / Retailer

The Application for Certificate of Registration (COR) for Wholesalers, Traders, Distributors, or Retailers in the vape industry is necessary to ensure legal compliance with local regulations.

The Certificate of Registration (COR) ensures that the business is officially recognized by regulatory authorities and can legally engage in the sale or distribution of vape products.

Obtaining the COR is necessary for all stakeholders in the vape supply chain to legally operate, distribute, or retail vape-related products. It ensures that businesses adhere to safety, health, and age-restriction laws, preventing underage access and promoting public health awareness.

The application process requires businesses to demonstrate that they meet specific legal, regulatory, and safety requirements established by relevant authorities. This includes providing accurate information about the nature of their operations, product offerings, and business practices.
