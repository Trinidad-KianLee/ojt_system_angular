Application for Promotional Events

All promotional activities involving vaporized nicotine and non-nicotine products must be pre-approved
by the Office in accordance with national regulatory standards.

The application for the conduct of Promotional Events involving vaporized nicotine and non-nicotine products is a critical component in ensuring regulatory compliance and responsible product promotion.

This process is designed to assess and authorize events that involve the marketing, showcasing, or distribution of such products to the public. By requiring prior approval, the Office ensures that all promotional activities meet legal standards, adhere to public health policies, and implement safeguards to prevent exposure to underage individuals.

Beyond compliance, the application system promotes transparency and accountability among event organizers. It also serves as a proactive measure to encourage responsible engagement with vaporized products, ensuring that all promotional efforts are conducted in a manner that prioritizes consumer safety, informed choice, and social responsibility.
