Application for Standards Organization Certification (SOC) and Certificate of Compliance to Regulations (CCR)

In order to legally manufacture, import, distribute, or sell products in the Philippines, businesses must ensure that their products meet the required safety, quality, and regulatory standards. The Standards Organization Certification (SOC) and Certificate of Compliance to Regulations (CCR) are two critical certifications issued by the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) to verify that products conform to these standards.

The SOC ensures that the product meets the Philippine National Standards (PNS) or other internationally recognized standards for quality, performance, and safety. It is issued after thorough evaluation and testing by accredited organizations.

The CCR verifies that the product complies with all specific regulations set by governing bodies such as the DTI, Food and Drug Administration (FDA), and other relevant authorities. This certification is necessary to ensure that the product is safe for consumer use and follows legal requirements for marketing and distribution.
